## About This Dashboard

This dashboard explores housing market data from King County, WA to understand how home prices vary across time, location, and property characteristics.

While the current view highlights a single selected year, users can interactively choose different years to perform year-over-year analysis and identify shifts in market behavior.

The dashboard helps answer:
- How do home prices change throughout the year?
- How do prices vary across different regions of King County?
- What price ranges dominate the market?
- How do home features such as size, bedrooms, bathrooms, view, and condition impact sale prices?

## About This Dashboard

This dashboard analyzes customer review data for British Airways to evaluate overall satisfaction and identify key drivers of passenger experience.

It examines how ratings vary over time, across regions, traveler types, seat classes, and aircraft models. The dashboard is designed to support data-driven decision-making for improving service quality and customer retention.

Users can interactively filter the data to explore specific time periods, customer segments, and service dimensions.

The dashboard helps answer:
- How has customer satisfaction changed over time?
- Which service areas perform well and which need improvement?
- How do ratings differ by region and traveler type?
- Which aircraft models receive better or worse reviews?

---

## Key Insights

- Overall average rating is relatively strong, but varies across service categories
- Cabin staff service performs better than entertainment and food services
- Customer ratings show increased volatility after 2020
- Certain aircraft models consistently receive higher ratings
- Regional and segment-based differences highlight uneven service quality
- Value-for-money and in-flight experience remain key improvement areas
